Conclusions¶

According to the above analysis we can find the below points :

We have about 23,26% of the reserved appointments not attend the appointment

There is no relation between working days or the week end and the no show , even we found the Tuesday and Wednesday show high variance in the reserved appointments with the same behavior of no show comparing with the other days ( high reservations with low no show )

Patients age above 40 years old showing high numbers of reserving appointments with low number of no show cases and the percentage increase with increasing of age stage

SMS notification had no impact on attending the appointment as its show the number of patients received SMS and not attend mush more than attending one

Other factor not show any impact in attending or not attending behavior , as its almost similar
